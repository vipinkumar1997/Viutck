Government Announces ₹10,000 Direct Relief Package for All Citizens

By Rajesh Kumar | Updated: June 13, 2026, 01:05 IST

NEW DELHI: In a landmark decision today, the Union Cabinet approved a new economic stabilization package designed to provide direct relief to citizens. Under the scheme, an amount of ₹10,000 will be credited directly to eligible accounts starting next week.

The initiative aims to offset recent inflationary pressures and assist middle-class households with rising healthcare and commodity costs. Treasury officials confirmed that the disbursement infrastructure is ready and public portals will open soon for applications.

Economists have largely welcomed the move, noting that a direct cash transfer of this scale will immediately boost consumer spending and stimulate market growth. Further guidelines and account registration steps are expected to be published later today.
